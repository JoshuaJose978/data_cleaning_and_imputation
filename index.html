How to Interpret These Results

Mean and Standard Deviation Differences:

These metrics show how well each method preserves the statistical properties of the original data. Values closer to 0 indicate better performance.

Distribution Similarity:

The KS statistic measures the maximum difference between the original and imputed distributions. Lower values (closer to 0) indicate better preservation of the original distribution.
